## About Us

Kona Hydrostatic Testing is a **DOT-authorized testing facility** for SCUBA
cylinders, O2 cylinders, fire extinguishers, SCBAs, paintball cylinders and
propane cylinders.

We also do **visual inspections** on scuba cylinders and **repair** of scuba
equipment.

Whether you need an annual visual, a five-year hydrostatic test, a valve
service, or a regulator rebuild, we can take care of it — and we're happy to
explain exactly what your cylinder needs and why.
